FAQ

How do I detect crop disease?

Go to Smart Features, tap AI Detector, upload a crop image, and click Detect.

How do I check market prices?

Open Live Market Prices, enter crop and location, then tap Check Price.

How do I use the farming chatbot?

Open Smart Farming Chatbot, type your crop or farming question, and tap Send for guidance.

What can I ask in the chatbot?

You can ask about crop diseases, prevention, fertilizers, irrigation, season-wise planning, and farm best practices.

Why is translation sometimes slower?

Some languages rely on best-effort translation paths and may take a little longer.
